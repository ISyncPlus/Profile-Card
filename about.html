About Me

A deeper look into my journey, goals, and reflections.

Bio

I'm Ebube, a frontend dev figuring out the full-stack path one project at a time. I wrapped up my SIWES program recently and I'm still glued to React, Tailwind, JavaScript, and a bit of Express. Most of what I know comes from building small things like a JS calculator and a little inventory manager, then breaking and fixing them until they feel right.

Goals in This Program

  • Ship more real projects so the full-stack workflow stops feeling like theory.
  • Stay consistent with practice, even when life gets loud.
  • Get comfortable enough to branch into Dart and Flutter when the time comes.

Areas of Low Confidence

I still trip over backend structure and performance debugging. When an API slows down or state blows up, I waste time guessing instead of knowing what to check. That's the muscle I'm trying to build right now.

Note to Future Self

Keep the pace steady. Ask why things work. Don't rush past the basics. Finish what you start, then move to the next thing. Dart and Flutter will be waiting when the foundation feels solid.

Extra Thoughts

When I'm not pushing commits, I'm probably catching up on anime, telling stories with friends, or getting lost in a game. Those breaks keep me sane and make the next line of code a little easier to write.
